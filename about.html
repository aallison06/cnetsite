About The CNET Class

Welcome to the CNET Class!

CNET, short for Computer Networking Electronics Technologies, is a career technical class led by Mr. Kristian Casey, a USAF Special Forces Veteran. Mr. Casey joined the Tri-Rivers team back in 2015. Since then, CNET has been an evolving class, introducing events like CyberPatriot and Esports to the curriculum.

Course Overview

The course spans two years and covers a wide range of topics. Students have the opportunity to earn various certifications, including:

  • CompTIA IT Fundamentals
  • CompTIA Network+
  • Fiber Optics Technician
  • IC3 Digital Literacy
  • Microsoft Azure
  • Various Programming Certifications

First Year of Curriculum

In the first year, known as Level One, students undergo certification training for CompTIA IT Fundamentals. This involves using the TestOut training course, owned by CompTIA. Various other training aspects will include:

  • Computer building
  • Hardware & software troubleshooting
  • Panel "punchdown" & RJ-45 termination
  • Basic programming
  • Binary & hexadecimal languages
  • Introduction to subnetting

Second Year of Curriculum

In the second year, known as Level Two, students undergo certification training for Fiber Optics. This involves using the training course provided by the Fiber Optics Association. Then, students will choose their path between Microsoft, Networking, and Cybersecurity certifications. Various other training aspects will be covered during the second year, including:

  • IP Breakdowns
  • IP Subnetting
  • Cisco Packet Tracer
  • Network Layers
  • Switch Configuration
  • Network Configuration
  • Network Protocols
  • Fiber Splicing
  • Fiber Termination
  • Fiber Safety
  • Fiber Configuration
  • Cybersecurity Basics
  • Cyber Safety
  • Threat Prevention

Towards the end of the year, Level One students prepare for the CompTIA IT Fundamentals certification exam. This will involve mainly taking practice exams through TestOut, to ensure that the student is well-prepared for the exam day. Mr. Casey will see to it that you are successful throughout your time at Tri-Rivers Career Center!
